# 📸 Dataset Contribution Guidelines (McFlourish)

Thank you for helping improve McFlourish! 🔥

We are building a **realistic indoor fire & smoke detection dataset**.  
Please follow these guidelines before submitting images.

---

## 🎯 What we need

We are looking for **indoor images** that reflect real environments where fire detection is useful:

- Rooms (living rooms, kitchens, bedrooms)
- Offices
- Hallways
- Garages
- Shops / public indoor spaces

---

## 🔥 Image categories

Please organize images into one of these categories:

- **fire** – visible flames
- **smoke** – visible smoke (even small amounts)
- **no_fire** – normal indoor scenes (no fire or smoke)

---

## ⚠️ Important requirements

### ✅ Realistic scenarios
- Images should look like **real surveillance situations**
- Avoid artificial or unrealistic scenes

### ❌ Avoid random images
- No landscapes, forests, or outdoor-only scenes
- No unrelated images (animals, memes, etc.)

### 🏠 Prefer indoor environments
- Indoor images are MUCH more valuable than outdoor ones

---

## 📦 Submission size

Please **do NOT send huge datasets at once**.

👉 Recommended:
- Around **50–150 images per submission**

This helps us:
- Review quality
- Maintain consistency
- Avoid unusable data

---

## 🧠 Quality tips

- Different lighting conditions (day/night)
- Different camera angles
- Different distances
- Real objects (candles, kitchens, electronics, etc.)

---

## 🚫 What to avoid

- Blurry images
- Extremely low resolution
- Duplicate images
- AI-generated images (unless clearly labeled)

---

## ⚖️ Permissions

By contributing, you confirm that:
- You have the right to share these images
- You allow their use in this project (non-commercial)

---

## 📬 How to contribute

- Open a Pull Request OR
- Contact via GitHub Issues

---

Thank you for helping build McFlourish! 🚀
